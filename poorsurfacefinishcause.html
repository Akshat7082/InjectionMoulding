Possible cause

Implementation on mould surface or inability of melt to replicate the mould contours.

Check for:

  1. Enlarge gates.
  2. Eliminate heavy bosses and ribes.
  3. Tool – polish, damage or rust marks.
  4. Injection speed – may be too low
  5. Mould temperature – may need to be increased
  6. Injection pressure – cavity may be under – pressurised
  7. Melt temperature – may need to increase.
