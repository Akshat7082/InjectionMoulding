Possible Cause for flash problem:

The mould is forced open due to the injection pressure being too high for the mould locking force or mould tool may be worn.

Check for flash problem:

  1. Reduce the material temperature.
  2. Reduce the parting line / matting.
  3. Mould clamping pressure – may be too low.
  4. Injection pressures may be too high for mould lock.
  5. Change over point from injection to ‘hold-on’ pressure is too late.
  6. Switches set to bring on mould lock before injection .
  7. Mould - for wear damage or an obstruction.
