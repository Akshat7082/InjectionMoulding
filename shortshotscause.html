Possible Cause

Insufficient Material injected into the cavity.

Check for:

  1. Increase Plastic raw material temperature.
  2. Increase number and size of vents.
  3. Correct unbalance condition.
  4. Short size
  5. Injection speed – may need to be increased
  6. Injection time – increase
  7. Changeover point from ‘Injection’ to ‘ Holding’ pressure
  8. Mould temperature - Increase
