Problem Cause

Material shrinkage or flowing back through the gate

Check for:

  1. Increase feed system.
  2. Increase injection pressure.
  3. Increase Mould and cylinder temperature.
  4. Hold – on pressure –may be too low
  5. Hold – on pressure –check levels.
  6. Material cushion or (pack) size.
  7. Mould temperature – may need to be too low
  8. Gate – may need to be increased.
