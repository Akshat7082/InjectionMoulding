Problem cause

Lack of fusion between cold fronts of material

Check for:

  1. Increase Gate size and Inject Slowly.
  2. Injection speed too low.
  3. Mould temperature – may be too low.
  4. Mould vents – may be blocked.
  5. Hold –on pressure may be too low.
  6. Shot volume – may be too small.
  7. Barrel temperature – may be too low.
