Possible Solution

  1. Avoid orientation.
  2. If possible, use follow up pressure lower than injection pressure.
  3. Shorten follow up pressure.
  4. Adjust melt Temperature (increase to relieve molded-in stress, decrease to avoid overpacking). stress, decrease to avoid over packing). stress, decrease to avoid over packing).
  5. Check gates for proper location and adequate size.
  6. Check mold knockout mechanism for proper design and operation.
  7. Equalize/balance mold temperature of both halves.
  8. Increase injection-hold.
  9. Increase mold cooling time.
  10. Relocate gates on or as near as possible to thick sections.
  11. Try increasing or decreasing injection pressure.
